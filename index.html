My name is Ricardo Martins, and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Coimbra (UC). I hold a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering, specializing in automation and robotics, with a background in Biomedical Engineering (MSc).

Throughout my research career at UC, particularly in laboratories like the Institute of Systems and Robotics (ISR-UC), the Coimbra Institute for Biomedical Imaging and Translational Research (CIBIT-UC), and the Institute of Nuclear Sciences Applied to Health (ICNAS-UC), my work has focused on two main areas: artificial perception in robotics and neuroimaging in cognitive neuroscience. These fields converge through the use of statistical analysis, multivariate signal processing, and computational modeling.

On this website, you can explore my curriculum vitae, browse my latest publications, delve into my main research topics, discover projects and demos, and find contact information.
