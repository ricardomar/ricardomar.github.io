Welcome !

My name is Ricardo Martins, and I hold a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a specialization in automation and robotics. I also have a background in Biomedical Engineering (MSc).

Throughout my research career at institutions such as the University of Coimbra (UC), the Institute of Systems and Robotics (ISR-UC), the Coimbra Institute for Biomedical Imaging and Translational Research (CIBIT-UC), and the Institute of Nuclear Sciences Applied to Health (ICNAS-UC), I have focused on two main areas of research and innovation. These include artificial perception in robotics and neuroimaging in cognitive neuroscience. Both domains intersect through the application of statistics and probability, multivariate signal processing, and computational modeling.

On this website, you can explore my curriculum vitae, browse through my latest publications, investigate my main research topics, explore open-source projects and demos, and find out how to contact me.
