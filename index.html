I am Ricardo Martins, a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Coimbra (UC). I hold a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering, specializing in automation and robotics, and an MSc in Biomedical Engineering.

During my PhD, my research focused on artificial perception models in robotics. Currently, as a postdoctoral researcher, I work in neuroimaging in cognitive neuroscience at UC, within the Coimbra Institute for Biomedical Imaging and Translational Research (CIBIT-UC) and the Institute of Nuclear Sciences Applied to Health (ICNAS-UC).

My core training and research interests include probability and statistics, multivariate signal and image processing, computational modeling, and machine intelligence, which I apply to investigate brain function using neuroimaging (PET, fMRI, MRI).

This website provides an overview of my work, including my curriculum vitae, publications, key research topics, projects and demos, and contact information.
