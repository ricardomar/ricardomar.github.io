--- layout: page title: Welcome description: "Ricardo Martins, postdoctoral researcher, CIBIT, ICNAS, UC, University of Coimbra" ---

My name is Ricardo Martins, and I hold a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a specialization in automation and robotics, complemented by an MSc in Biomedical Engineering.

Over the course of my research career at institutions like the University of Coimbra (UC), the Institute of Systems and Robotics (ISR-UC), the Coimbra Institute for Biomedical Imaging and Translational Research (CIBIT-UC), and the Institute of Nuclear Sciences Applied to Health (ICNAS-UC), my work has primarily focused on two key areas: artificial perception in robotics and neuroimaging in cognitive neuroscience. These fields intersect through the use of statistical analysis, multivariate signal processing, and computational modeling.

On this website, you can explore my curriculum vitae, browse my latest publications, delve into my main research topics, discover projects and demos, and find contact information.
